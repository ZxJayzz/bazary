## III. EXCESSIVE PROVISIONS ANALYSIS / ANALYSE DES DISPOSITIONS EXCESSIVES

### English

After thorough review, the following provisions were assessed for potential excessiveness:

#### 1. Article 12 - Indemnification: APPROPRIATE
The indemnification clause covers CGU violations, platform misuse, illicit content publication, and user-to-user transactions. This is **standard practice** for marketplace platforms worldwide. The scope is limited to user-caused damages and does not extend to unreasonable scenarios. **Verdict: Not excessive.**

#### 2. Article 13 - Force Majeure: APPROPRIATE
The force majeure list (natural disasters, power outages, pandemics, civil unrest, government decisions) is particularly **relevant to Madagascar** where cyclones, power outages, and telecom interruptions are common occurrences. **Verdict: Not excessive, well-adapted to local context.**

#### 3. Article 14 - CGU Modification: MARGINALLY AGGRESSIVE
The right to modify CGU "at any time" with acceptance-by-continued-use is standard for web platforms. However, it lacks a **minimum notice period** before changes take effect. This is a common practice but could be strengthened by adding a 15-30 day notice period for substantive changes, which would better align with consumer protection principles.
**Verdict: Acceptable but could be improved. Low risk.**

#### 4. Article 9 (Privacy) - IP License on User Content: APPROPRIATE
The non-exclusive license granted to Bazary for user-published images and content is limited to the scope of the Service. This is **necessary for platform operation** (displaying listings, thumbnails, search results). **Verdict: Not excessive.**

#### 5. Article 10 (CGU) - Limitation of Liability: APPROPRIATE
The limitation covers failed transactions, damages from platform use, user-published content, and technical service interruptions. These are all areas where Bazary legitimately has limited control as an intermediary. **Verdict: Not excessive, consistent with intermediary status.**

Apres examen approfondi, les dispositions suivantes ont ete evaluees pour exces potentiel :

La clause d'indemnisation couvre les violations des CGU, l'utilisation abusive de la plateforme, la publication de contenus illicites et les transactions entre utilisateurs. C'est une **pratique standard** pour les plateformes de marche dans le monde entier. #### 5. Article 10 (CGU) - Limitation de responsabilite : APPROPRIEE
La limitation couvre les transactions echouees, les dommages lies a l'utilisation de la plateforme, le contenu publie par les utilisateurs et les interruptions techniques.
